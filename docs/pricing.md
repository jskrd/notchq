# Pricing Model

## Subscription Tiers

### Free - $0/month

- 1 offering
- Unlimited bookings
- Unlimited staff/users
- **5% transaction fee on paid bookings**
- "Powered by NotchQ" branding (cannot be removed)
- Email notifications only

### Pro - $29/month

- Unlimited offerings
- Unlimited bookings
- Unlimited staff/users
- **2% transaction fee on paid bookings**
- Remove branding + custom branding (logo, colors)
- Email + SMS notifications
- Priority support

## Strategy Notes

- **Transaction fee model** - Revenue aligned with customer success
- Free tier has higher transaction fee (5%) + forced branding
- Pro tier reduces transaction fee to 2% + removes branding
- No per-seat pricing - unlimited users encourages team adoption
- Simple 2-tier model to start - can add Enterprise tier later based on customer
  demand

## Future Considerations

- Payment processing markup (0.5-1% on top of Stripe)
- Higher commission on add-ons (10-15%)
- Volume-based pricing adjustments based on usage patterns
- Additional tier based on customer demand (multiple businesses, API access,
  reduced/no transaction fees)
